Rational mNAV · BTC-denominated Monte Carlo

MSTR ⇄ BTC Decision Engine

Rational mNAV — valuing the wrapper in Bitcoin terms against the self-custody hurdle. Holding your own coins is the benchmark: one BTC stays one BTC, a 0% Bitcoin-denominated return. The tool asks whether owning MSTR beats that, after a counterparty-risk hurdle — an annual rate that compounds over your horizon and that the equity must clear in Bitcoin terms. Your job is to set the horizon, the hurdle, and your Bitcoin price model. The tool runs a single Monte Carlo over the Bitcoin price (four models to choose from) and the policy rate, propagating Strategy's full capital structure with the Bitcoin Per Share of Common Stock (BPS) and the common stock mNAV premium computed endogenously along every path. mNAV here is MSTR's equity market cap (share price × diluted shares) over the dollar value of its Bitcoin. The results also report two capital-structure views of that same fair value — the enterprise mNAV (the whole capital stack, equity plus preferreds and convertibles net of the cash reserve, over gross Bitcoin) and the net-assets mNAV (equity over the Bitcoin backing that remains after those senior claims). The assumptions fields are pre-filled with a snapshot you can edit to match current values.
